2. Flex-container的属性
   - flex-direction
   - flex-wrap
   - flex-flow
   - justify-content
   - align-items
   - Align-content

flex-direction`默认为row。row-reverse就是倒序排列。column是按竖直从上往下排列

- `flex-wrap`用来指定是否要换行，默认是不换行，换行需要`flex-wrap: wrap`
